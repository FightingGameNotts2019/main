BYTE FIGHTER GAME

created by: 
		Alexander Unsworth-Tomlinson 
		Gurpreet Ark, 
		Mohammed Rahman 
		and Stepan Romanov.

Byte Fight is a 2D 2-player fighting game where players attack the other player while avoiding being hit themselves, there are a variety of levels but be sure to watch your step! As it could cost you your life!
